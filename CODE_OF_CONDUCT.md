# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

Examples of behavior that help build a healthy community:

- being respectful in disagreements
- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent while staying precise
- keeping discussions focused on the work

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insults
- doxxing or sharing private information
- disruptive trolling, bad-faith review behavior, or spam

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, lock threads, or ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

For non-sensitive conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository.

For sensitive or safety-related concerns, do not open a public issue. Contact the maintainers privately through the repository owner or organization profile.
